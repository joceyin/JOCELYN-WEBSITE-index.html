Pursuit Of Happiness

This is one of my favorite podcasts, titled “Pursuit Of Happiness” from Pancatera Podcast. From this video, I gained some new insights that opened my mind. They are talking about what is the definition of happiness, how do you deal with strong emotions, try to manage the reaction, not the feeling, etc. Some of the points I got from this podcast video are: As we get older, our priority to maintain happiness and peace comes first. Don't share your energy on things that are unnecessary and will eventually become draining. Putting yourself first, your energy first, and only saying yes and agreeing to things that you want to do that makes you happy.
